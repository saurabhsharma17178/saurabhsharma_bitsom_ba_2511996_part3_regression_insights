## Business Interpretation

Positive residuals indicate that actual sales were higher than predicted. These stores may have benefited from factors such as local promotions, seasonal demand, strong management, or customer loyalty that are not included in the model.

Negative residuals indicate that actual sales were lower than predicted. Possible reasons include operational issues, stronger local competition, inventory shortages, or lower customer demand.

## Model Evaluation

The model captures several important business drivers but does not explain all variation in monthly sales. Additional variables such as promotions, seasonality, and local economic conditions could improve prediction accuracy.
